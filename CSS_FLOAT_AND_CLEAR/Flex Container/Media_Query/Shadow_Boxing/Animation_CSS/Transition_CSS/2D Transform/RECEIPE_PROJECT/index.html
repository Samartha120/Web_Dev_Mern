Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • 5ml of water
  • chicken or goat meat
  • salt
  • green chilli
  • Biryani Masala
  • Rice

Receipe

  1. Place pot with chicken in it onto a stove over medium heat. Cover and cook for 5 minutes.Remove lid. Cook for 5 minutes, turning chicken twice.
  2. Remove from heat.
  3. Turn chicken so skin side is down – it should cover most of the base of the pot.
  4. Scatter over half the onion then half the coriander.
  5. Top with all the rice. Gently pat down and flatten surface.
  6. Drizzle saffron across rice surface in random pattern, then drizzle over ghee.
  7. Place lid on. Return to stove over medium heat
  8. As soon as you see steam, turn down to low then cook for 25 minutes.
  9. Remove from stove, rest with lid on for 10 minutes.
  10. Your Biryani is ready
